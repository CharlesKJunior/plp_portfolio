About Me

Mutebwa Charles is passionate about leveraging technology for community empowerment and addressing social issues. He is a second-year Computer Science and Information Technology student at Uganda Martyrs University, where he combines his technical skills with leadership roles to make an impact.

Moreover, Charles is an advocate for technological innovation and has demonstrated a commitment to expanding his own technical knowledge. His Power Learn Project Scholarship from PLP Africa in January 2025 highlights his potential in technology and his continuous drive for professional development. While he is still early in his career, his dedication to combining technology with social good sets him apart as someone who aspires to use tech solutions for large-scale societal impact.
